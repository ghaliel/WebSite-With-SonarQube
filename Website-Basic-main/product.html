Mercedes S65 Cabriolet AMG

Type: W290

from USD 132.999 $

With the new S 65 Cabriolet, Mercedes-AMG is for the first time offering a performance car which combines the impressive power of the 6.0 litre V12 biturbo engine with the pleasure of open-top driving for four occupants. An output of 463 kW (630 hp), 1000 newton metres of maximum torque, the sport suspension based on AIRMATIC and the exquisite appointments emphasise the exceptional status of the new flagship model in the S-Class family from Mercedes-AMG. It offers both luxury and performance, this way you can rush your kids to school in complete class. According to the manufacturer, the average consumption is 13.0 l/100km (1 in 13.1).
